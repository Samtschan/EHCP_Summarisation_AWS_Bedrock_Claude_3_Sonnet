Emily, a 14-year-old with Autism Spectrum Disorder and Anxiety Disorder, excels in visual tasks but struggles with attention and abstract reasoning. Educationally, she performs above average in visual-spatial tasks but below average in sustained attention tests. Socially, she finds group interactions challenging due to difficulties understanding social cues, but has a supportive family network. With therapy and accommodations, Emily has made progress in managing her anxiety and developing coping strategies. Her family hopes she can achieve her dream of becoming a graphic designer.
